## 4. Communication Plan

### 4.2 Communication Timeline
```
Week 1: Announcement
├── Executive sponsor message
├── FAQ document published
└── Feedback channel established

Week 2-4: Awareness
├── Department meetings
├── Intranet updates
└── Q&A sessions

Week 5-8: Training Announcements
├── Training schedule published
├── Registration opened
└── Pre-training materials distributed

Week 9-12: Go-Live Communications
├── Daily updates during rollout
├── Support resources promoted
└── Success stories shared

Post Go-Live: Reinforcement
├── Tips and tricks
├── User feedback collection
└── Lessons learned
```

## 6. Resistance Management

### 6.1 Anticipated Resistance
| Source | Resistance Type | Root Cause | Mitigation Strategy |
|--------|-----------------|------------|---------------------|
| Sys Admins | Active | Workflow disruption | Involve in design, training |
| End Users | Passive | Inconvenience | Clear communication, support |
| Management | Skeptical | Cost concerns | ROI demonstration |
| Legacy Team | Active | Job security fears | Skill development, reassignment |

### 6.2 Resistance Response Plan
| Resistance Level | Indicators | Response Actions |
|------------------|------------|------------------|
| Low | Questions, concerns voiced | Address in communication |
| Medium | Delays, workarounds | One-on-one coaching |
| High | Refusal, escalation | Leadership intervention |
| Critical | Sabotage, public opposition | Immediate escalation |

### 6.3 Change Champions

|------|------------|------|------------------|

**Champion Responsibilities:**
- Advocate for the change within their department
- Provide feedback from stakeholders
- Support colleagues through the transition
- Identify and escalate resistance
- Celebrate successes

## 10. Risk Management

### 10.1 Change-Related Risks
| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Low adoption | Medium | High | Champion network, incentives |
| Knowledge loss | Low | High | Documentation, cross-training |
| Productivity dip | High | Medium | Training, support increase |
| Resistance escalation | Medium | Medium | Early engagement, communication |

### 10.2 Contingency Plans
| Scenario | Trigger | Response |
|----------|---------|----------|
| Mass resistance | > 30% non-compliance | Executive intervention, timeline extension |
| Training failure | < 70% completion | Additional sessions, mandatory enrollment |
| Support overwhelm | > 200 tickets/day | Additional temporary staff |
| Go-live issues | Critical defects | Rollback plan activation |
